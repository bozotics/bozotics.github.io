One of the most prestigious robotics competition in Singapore with over 60,000 students taking part in it. The champion of each category has the opportunity to represent Singapore in the World Robotic Olympiad. We are aiming for the championship award which is determined based on a group's robot performance, programming, mechanical design and presentation skill.
